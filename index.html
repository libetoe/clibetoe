Who I Am

I'm a cybersecurity professional and full-stack developer based in Maseru, Lesotho, currently serving as SOC Analyst at the Central Bank of Lesotho. My day-to-day revolves around monitoring enterprise infrastructure, triaging security alerts, investigating incidents, and hunting for advanced persistent threats using SIEM tooling and log correlation.

Beyond the SOC, I bring a developer's perspective — I've built production web applications in Laravel, Django, and Flask, which gives me a unique understanding of how attackers exploit software vulnerabilities from the inside out.

I've competed at continental level, winning 1st place in Attack & Defence and 1st place in Social Engineering at the AfricaCERT CTF Hackathon 2025 — one of Africa's premier cybersecurity competitions. That experience sharpened my offensive mindset and reinforced why defensive operations must think like attackers.
